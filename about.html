🌟 About TV Tracker

Your Ultimate TV Companion

TV Tracker is a modern platform built for TV series enthusiasts who want to organize and personalize their watching experience. Track every show, discover new releases, and never lose track of what to watch next.

🎬

What We Do

TV Tracker was created with one goal in mind: give TV lovers a clean, powerful tool to manage their watching life. We believe that tracking your shows should be effortless — no spreadsheets, no sticky notes, just a beautiful app that does the work for you.

From binge-watching the latest Netflix original to rewatching a beloved classic, TV Tracker helps you organize everything in one place. Add shows with detailed information including cast, creators, genre, platform, and release dates. Our calendar view gives you a clear picture of what's coming up, so you never miss a premiere.

Your data is stored securely in the cloud with Supabase, and the entire experience is designed to be fast, intuitive, and visually stunning. Whether you're a casual viewer or a hardcore binge-watcher, TV Tracker is built for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TV Tracker free to use? ▾

Yes, TV Tracker is completely free to use. Create an account and start tracking your shows immediately with no hidden fees or premium tiers.

Do I need an account to use TV Tracker? ▾

Yes, you need a free account to access the Tracker, Calendar, and Home pages. This allows us to securely store your data in the cloud and sync it across devices.

Can I upload images for my shows? ▾

Absolutely. You can upload poster images for each show, platform logos, and profile photos for creators and cast members. All images are stored securely in the cloud.

What platforms can I track? ▾

You can track shows from any platform — Netflix, HBO Max, Apple TV+, Disney+, Crunchyroll, Hulu, Amazon Prime Video, BBC, and more. If it exists, you can track it.

How does the calendar work? ▾

The calendar displays release dates for all your tracked shows. Shows with a release date are automatically plotted on the calendar. You can also manually add upcoming shows directly from the calendar view.

Is my data private and secure? ▾

Yes. Your data is stored using Supabase with industry-standard encryption. We never sell or share your personal information. Only you can access your tracked shows and account details.

Can I delete my account? ▾

Yes, you can request account deletion at any time by reaching out through our Feedback form. Your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
